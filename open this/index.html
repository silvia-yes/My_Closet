Something in Writing!

Our group decided to create a virtual closet where we can visualize color combinations without having to go to malls/stores to try things on. In our closet we can model clothing on male/female avatars. We can customize many aspects within our web application, such as the gender, the skin color, the types of clothing, and the color of the clothing that we’re putting onto the avatar. Once we customize our avatar, we can save the image into a database. Additionally, when an outfit with its clothing and colors are chosen, the user can make a Google search for the outfit in case the user wants to buy the outfit online.
